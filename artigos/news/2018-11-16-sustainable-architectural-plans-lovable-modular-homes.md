We present projects on urban planning with systems of protecting environment and constructions with innovations and technology of renewable energies. And also constructions of sustainable homes, fixed or mobile modules, innovation and technology for energy saving and long duration.

BAP Homes are ideal for residences, schools, commercial or military use, with options thermal coating of last generation that ensures a temperature between 15-18 degrees celsius, special coating indestructibility of natural climatic action or anti-impact coating.

Modular sustainable constructions are environment-friendly homes, tough, safer, durable, smart, adaptable, economic and of easy motion. Ideal homes for any kind of weather, perfect for city, mountain, desert and jungle.

BAP Homes offer the latest in sustainable technology and important equipment for the energy savings. Its modular structure based on shipping containers allows mobility. The use of shipping containers as structural material for building homes is more practical and montage time is reduced by up to 60%. This type of structural unit meets perfectly with the current need to minimize environmental impact.

Smart and sustainable homes based modular shipping containers. The container-based modules homes offer a very strong structure and they are prepared to risk areas, conflict zones and meet with credits to any type of residential social project.

Product made from Norway, quality guarantees and durability for generations, structural: 100 years, thermal insulation: 50 years acoustic insulation: 50 years, online sales and shipments anywhere, taxes and transport not included.
