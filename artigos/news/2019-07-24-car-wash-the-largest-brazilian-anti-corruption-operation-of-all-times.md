On March 17, 2014, Federal Police of Brazil launched an operation that would later be known as Lava Jato (Car Wash), which fused four investigations that scrutinize financial crimes and misappropriation of public funds.

The operations were named Dolce Vita, Bidone, Casablanca and Lava Jato, however while the first three correspond to classic movie titles, chosen according to the individual profile of each money-maker, the name Lava Jato refers to a network of laundries and  a gas station in Brasilia that was used by one of the criminal organizations, initially investigated for illicit financial flows (IFFs).

At first, it was identified the main characters of the clandestine foreign exchange market act in the criminal scheme investigated in Brazil. These money changers were responsible for the financial movement and money laundering of countless individuals and legal entities, which ultimately involved a wide range of other crimes, such as international drug trafficking, corruption of public officials, tax evasion, currency evasion, illegal extraction and smuggling of precious stones, misappropriation of public funds, among others.

Originally, the Financial Activities Control Board (COAF / MF) reported that the group carried out atypical financial operations exceeding R $ 10 billion. The advance of the investigation revealed that the money-makers also facilitated the transfer of bribes adjusted to the detriment of the Federal Public Administration and resulting from public contracts concluded through fraud in bidding processes.

The analysis of the material seized in the various searches determined by the Federal Court demonstrated that the political indication of public agents that promoted and facilitated the fraudulent hiring of goods and services were overpriced. As stated by people investigated and collaborative defendants, there was also the formation of cartels in numerous economic sectors, which caused serious financial damage to contracting public companies. Fraud in bidding processes was carried out to allow contracting by contractors involved in the criminal scheme with many Petrobras departments.

The possible involvement of public and parliamentary agents, who enjoy a special forum for public function prerogative, led the Federal Police to reinforce a working group that acts in higher courts. This team is responsible for developing judicial police actions before higher courts: STF and STJ. The progression of police investigations conducted to the trigger of several other phases of Car Wash Operation. Convictions, supported by robust evidence produced by Brazilian Federal Police, naturally resulted from the unequivocal finding which revealed a vast scheme of corruption and misappropriation of public funds, a case with no parallel in Brazilian History.
