Silvio Santos, whose real name is Senor Abravanel, was born on December 12, 1930, in Rio de Janeiro and died on August 17, 2024 in São Paulo at the age of 93. He was one of Brazil's most iconic presenters and entrepreneurs, known for his vast contribution to Brazilian television and the empire he built over decades.

### Early career

Silvio began his career selling pens on the streets of Rio de Janeiro, where he already demonstrated his charisma and communication skills. His ability to engage people led him to opportunities in radio, where he began working as an announcer. His natural talent made him migrate to television, where he began to gain notoriety as a presenter.

### Contribution to Communication

Silvio Santos is widely recognized as one of the greatest communicators in Brazil. He founded *SBT (Brazilian Television System)* in 1981, consolidating himself as one of the main names in Brazilian television. Under his leadership, [SBT](https://www.sbt.com.br/) became one of the largest television networks in the country, known for its diverse programming, which includes soap operas, auditorium programs, and entertainment shows.

The program "Programa Silvio Santos", on the air since the 60s, is one of the most distant on Brazilian television. Silvio Santos innovated by creating his own style of presenting, mixing entertainment with audience participation, something that was fundamental to the format of auditorium programs in Brazil.

### As an entrepreneur

In addition to his work as a presenter, Silvio Santos is a successful businessman. His business group, the *Silvio Santos Group*, includes several companies, such as Baú da Felicidade, Lojas do Baú, Jequiti Cosméticos, and Tele Sena. He was also the owner of Banco Panamericano.

Silvio's entrepreneurial vision was crucial for the growth of SBT, which became one of Rede Globo's main competitors, something that seemed impossible at the time. His ability to reinvent himself and to understand what the public wanted to see on television was essential to the continued success of SBT and its other companies.

Silvio Santos is a legendary figure not only for his contribution to the media, but also as an example of entrepreneurship, being one of the few entrepreneurs who knew how to balance business success with a positive and charismatic public image.

### Legacy

Silvio Santos left an indelible mark on Brazilian culture. He is revered for both his role as a communicator and his business success, and he remains a highly respected and admired figure in Brazil.
