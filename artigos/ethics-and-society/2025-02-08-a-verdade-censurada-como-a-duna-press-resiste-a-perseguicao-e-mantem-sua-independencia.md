# **The Censored Truth: How Duna Press Resists Persecution and Maintains Its Independence**

While millions of dollars from American taxpayers are diverted to sustain these operations, the truth is systematically suppressed and replaced by controlled narratives.

In this context, *Duna Press Journal & Magazine* proudly reaffirms: we have never received a single cent from USAID or any other entity involved in this scheme. We are a truly independent press, composed of fair and honorable writers, committed to facts without distortions or manipulations.

And we, at *Duna Press Journal & Magazine*, will continue to be at the forefront, bringing free and honest information to those who refuse to be manipulated.
